[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
Pour a little more time into this glass tonight
Got nowhere to be but lost in your eyes
Every shadow is painting your silhouette right
Let the rest of the world just fade and pass by

[Verse 1]
The fire is low but the embers still glow
Your touch is a whisper that I want to know
We left the loud streets a long mile away
Just you and me finding the words we can't say

[Pre-Chorus]
The closer you pull, the deeper I fall
I am caught in the gravity, risking it all

Is telling us simply to let the night pass

[Chorus]
So make it last, stretch out the midnight
Let every second burn slow in the warm light
I am holding you close, breathing you in
Right where the end of the evening begins
